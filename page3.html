Eat, drink, rest

It's like portable table. PorTable! Get it? :D

Comfy Space basically could be describe as an armrest and a stand for drinks. Something that each of us associates and met with in a car or cinema. Our product, however, is something more - we have redefined this simple piece of furniture so that it can be used in various ways and in many other places. You will be surprised how often it can be useful.

Use however and wherever you want!
There is a lot of possibilities to use our product. Both when it comes to the way it is used and the place where you put it:

On your couch

It's probably obvious that the couch is the best place to use it, right? Whether watching a movie or a match, you can enjoy your favorite beer and chips in a very safe and convenient way. Believe us - in a veeeeery convenient way.

On your bed

Since it can be used in various places, it would be foolish to not use it in bed. Well, let us not kid ourselves, the bed is a synonym of comfortable space and let it stay like that. With the addition of our product of course :)

On the floor

We have given this to testing probably the most difficult users who exist - children. And you know what? We have reduced the risk of washing carpets from "accidental" drink stains. And after all the fun you can hide a lot of toys inside and start living in blissful peace and quiet.


And much more, like:

- the back sit of your car,
- at the console where there is not enough space for a table, and you do not want to spill drinks,
- as an additional table when you play board games that occupy the entire table top ,
- when you rest on the balcony enjoying the sun ,
- in a chill room in a company, where it not only looks great and perfectly fits chair beanbags,
We have tested all of the above and we can confirm the usefulness of ComfySpace. We are sure that in the future we will be surprised at how users will use it. Maybe we will even make a contest with prizes for the most intriguing way of using our product :)
